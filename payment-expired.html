The payment window has closed. Cryptocurrency prices fluctuate, so payment addresses expire after 30 minutes for your protection.

What happened?

When you start a crypto payment, we lock in the exchange rate and generate a unique payment address. This address and rate are valid for 30 minutes. If no payment is received in that time, the order expires to prevent issues with price changes.

  • No funds were charged
  • You can start a new order anytime
  • The new order will use current exchange rates
Did you send payment before expiration?
If you sent cryptocurrency but the order expired, don't worry. Contact us at support@tradingbrainz.com with your transaction ID and we'll resolve it.
