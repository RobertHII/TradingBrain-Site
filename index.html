Important

Before You Purchase

You Build Your Edge

TradingBrainz provides the tools - you provide the strategy. The system comes bare bones with no pre-configured strategies. It's up to you to:

  • Train the AI with quality content
  • Design and refine your setups
  • Build your own trading approach
  • Manage your risk appropriately

This is not a "get rich quick" solution. Success depends entirely on your effort and skill.

API Keys Required

TradingBrainz is pre-wired for external service connections. You must provide and pay for your own API access:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) - Required for strategy extraction. Usage-based pricing.
  • QuantConnect - Required for backtesting. Free tier available.
  • Tradovate - Required for live bot trading. Broker account needed.

The system is natively built for Anthropic/Claude. At purchase, you configure your API keys and the system handles the rest.

FAQ

Common Questions

What do I need to get started?
Windows 10/11, Python 3.10+, and your own API keys (Anthropic for AI, QuantConnect for backtesting, Tradovate for live trading). 8GB RAM minimum, 16GB recommended.
Is this a signals service or trading bot?
Neither. TradingBrainz is a toolkit that helps you extract strategies from educational content and execute them with proper risk management. You develop your own strategies - the system just helps automate the workflow.
What happens after I purchase the Bundle?
You get immediate access to the Trading Brain. You have 7 days to select and activate your 2 lifetime Trading Bots. Bots not activated within 7 days are forfeited - this cannot be extended.
Can I use a different AI besides Claude?
The system is natively built for Anthropic/Claude. At purchase, you can specify your preferred AI and the system will be configured accordingly, but Claude provides the best results.
