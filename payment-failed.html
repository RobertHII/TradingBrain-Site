Payment Failed

We couldn't process your payment. Don't worry - no funds have been taken from your account.

This might have happened because:

  • The payment window timed out (crypto not sent within 30 minutes)
  • Insufficient amount was sent to the payment address
  • The transaction was sent to the wrong address
  • Network congestion caused delays beyond the payment window
  • The cryptocurrency network rejected the transaction

Already sent payment?

If you've already sent cryptocurrency and are seeing this error, please don't panic. Contact our support team at support@tradingbrainz.com with your transaction hash (TXID) and we'll investigate and process your order manually if the payment was received.
